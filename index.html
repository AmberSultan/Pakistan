Landmarks of Legacy

Tomb of Shah Rukn e Alam
MULTAN

The shrine of Rukn-e-Alam is one of southern Punjab's most important Sufi shrines.

Minare-e-Pakistan
LAHORE

Minar e Pakistan was constructed in the 1960s to commemorate the adoption of Pakistan Resolution of 23rd March 1940 at its site.

Faisal Mosque
ISLAMABAD

The Faisal Mosque, envisioned by King Faisal in 1966, inspired a global design competition in 1969.

Lahore Fort
LAHORE

The Fort is symbolic of the height of Mughal glory in medieval South Asia. The Lahore Fort is also known as Shahi Qila or Royal Fort.

Badshahi Mosque
LAHORE

The mosque is an important example of Mughal architecture,decorated with carved red sandstone with marble inlay.
